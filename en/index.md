# Uranium Diff Support

**Uranium Diff** is a **native macOS app** to view and understand the differences between files, with a modern interface that's **private by design**. It's built for developers, technical writers, and anyone who needs to see exactly what changed between two versions of a file, folder, or Git branch.

> One non-negotiable principle: **your file contents never leave your Mac.**

### What you can do

**Compare almost anything**
- **Text and code** with syntax highlighting (Swift, Python, JavaScript, and many more), side-by-side or unified (inline).
- **Structural comparison** of JSON, XML, plist, and YAML: key order isn't treated as a difference; tree view.
- **PDF** and **Word (.docx)**: extracted-text comparison.
- **Excel (.xlsx)**: cell-by-cell comparison across sheets.
- **Images** (PNG, JPEG, HEIC, and more): side-by-side, swipe slider, or onion-skin overlay, with changed regions highlighted.

**From wherever you work**
- **Local** files (dialog or drag from Finder).
- A **web URL** (HTTP/HTTPS).
- Remote servers over **SSH**, with connections stored securely in the Keychain.
- **Full folders** (local↔local, local↔SSH, SSH↔SSH), with file copy between sides and a conflict policy.

**Merge and export**
- **2-way and 3-way** merge with conflict resolution.
- **Git integration**: use it as a `difftool` and `mergetool`, and compare branches or commits without leaving the app.
- Export diff reports to **PDF** and **HTML**.

**Apple Intelligence, on your device**
- Change summaries, hunk explanations, and branch analysis generated **on-device** with Apple's local models. Your content never goes to the cloud.
- *AI-generated content may contain errors — always verify it.*

### Compatibility & requirements
| | |
|---|---|
| Operating system | **macOS 26 (Tahoe) or later** |
| Architecture | **Apple Silicon** (Mac with M1 chip or later) |
| Apple Intelligence | Requires **Apple Intelligence enabled** in System Settings (included on supported Macs). |
| Languages | Spanish and English · light & dark mode |
| Internet connection | Only to compare from a **URL** or over **SSH**. Local comparison and AI work offline. |

### Plans & pricing
- **Free (forever):** compare 2 local text/code files (side-by-side, inline, syntax highlighting), structural JSON/XML/plist/YAML diff, minimap and change navigation, statistics, copy the unified diff, compare from a URL, and comparison history.
- **Uranium Diff Pro (subscription):** folders, SSH, office formats (PDF/Word/Excel/images), 2-way & 3-way merge, Git integration, Apple Intelligence, report export, and the `uraniumdiff` CLI.
- **Trial:** every install includes **14 days** of all Pro features, **no card, no account**. After that, the app keeps working on the free plan.
- Pro is an **auto-renewing subscription** (monthly or yearly), managed from your App Store account settings.
