# Privacy policy

**Last updated:** July 13, 2026 · **Applies to:** Uranium Diff for macOS v1.0.0

**Summary:** Uranium Diff **collects no personal data**, **doesn't track**, and **uses no analytics**. Your file contents are processed **on your Mac** and are **never** sent to our servers (in fact, we run no servers that receive your content).

### Data we collect
**None.** No accounts, no sign-up, and no collection of personal data, usage data, or identifiers. This matches the "Data Not Collected" App Store privacy label and the app's privacy manifest.

### How your files are handled
The contents of the files you compare are processed **locally** on your device to compute and display differences. They are not transmitted to us or to third parties.

### Apple Intelligence (on-device AI)
AI features (summaries, explanations, branch analysis) use Apple's **local models (Foundation Models)** and run **on your Mac**. Your file contents do **not** leave the device for these features.

### Network use
The app only makes outbound connections that **you** initiate:
- **Downloading from a URL** you type (HTTP/HTTPS): fetched directly from that server.
- **SSH connections** to servers **you** configure: communication is direct with that host.

There is no first-party server, no telemetry, and no "phone home."

### Credentials & local storage
- **SSH credentials** are stored only in the macOS **Keychain**.
- Folder access is preserved via macOS **security-scoped bookmarks**.
- Files downloaded from a URL or over SSH go to a **temporary cache** that's cleared when you quit the app.

### Sandbox & permissions
The app runs in the macOS **App Sandbox** with minimal permissions: read/write for files **you** select, access to the **Downloads** folder, and outbound **network client**. It only accesses what you explicitly authorize.

### Purchases
Pro subscriptions are processed by **Apple (App Store / StoreKit)**. We do **not** receive or store payment data; subscription management happens with your **Apple ID**. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Third-party components
Uranium Diff bundles open-source libraries (e.g., syntax highlighting and file utilities) that run **locally** and do **not** transmit data.

### Children
The app is rated **4+** and collects no data from anyone, including children.

### Changes to this policy
If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here with its date. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.

### Contact
Privacy questions? Email **uraniumdiff@outlook.com**.
